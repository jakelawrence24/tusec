About

The purpose of this organization shall be to gather a growing group of students interested in the IT security field. By incorporating various interactive workshops, guess speaker lectures, and capture-the-flag events, students will have many takeaways with each semester. Workshops will allow for each student to implement and demonstrate new concepts, while guest speakers

will contribute with real world experience. By attending and even hosting capture-the-flag events, students can test their knowledge and get even more hands-on experience. With the growing number of students becoming interested in this field, TUsec will be a great start for the beginner and the advanced. Paperwork is in progress.
